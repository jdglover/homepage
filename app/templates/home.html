My interests

As a phonologist, I investigate how sounds pattern in the languages of the world. I am interested in using my expertise to develop innovative approaches to technical, language related problems. Recently I have begun working with Python and the Natural Language Toolkit in order to begin incorporating computational tools into my linguistics projects.

Outside of phonology I have experience in many linguistic subfields, including laboratory phonology/phonetics, morphology, syntax, pragmatics, and second language studies.

I can help you with a range of projects including naming, transcription, annotation, spectrogram analysis, stemming, and many more. If you have a linguistics related problem or project that you would like some help with, feel free to contact me!
